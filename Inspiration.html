A list of INSPIRATIONS to me and why

(i do this because I wish more artists did this)

Sculpture

Julian Miholics
super cool drawings and ceramics, fun shapes and colours and very unique ideas spatially.

Ollie Miles
Very creative book sculptures, wonderfully textured drawings, a real treat for the eyes.

Marla Hlady
pretty much the closest thing to a mentor I have, and her sculptural work is very cool and mechanical, (I wanna know what the bubble one is but there's no documentation)

Animation/Music

Averi
Some really spectacular experimental animation, an inspiration to be sure.

sodikken
very unique animation style, creating whimsical worlds filled with life and unique interactions

Tee Ken Ng
Lots of animation, often practically or using stop motion. quite experimental personality

Justine King
Some good wholesome animation, dealing out some very beautiful visuals. particularly their short film The Little Poet

Austin Curzon
A wonderful animator, very collaborative.

Bryson McBee
Some animation, some illustration, some music, even some game development, lots of blender models and charm.

Louie Zong
Illustrator, Musician, Animator, etc... very funky works and beats

Ian Worthington
Wonderful animator and musician, animations full of silly little guys

Toby Fox
Creator of Undertale and an inspiration for story and music.

temmie
Pixel artist extraordinaire for undertale and a wonderful animator to beat

Emily Xu
Beautiful animation and illustration, sparkles and shiny vibes.

Michelle
Similar to emily, animation and illustration with sharp edged and defined shapes, yest soft in tone and colour

AROURA
Inspiring music and a powerful empathetic soul.

Grant Wooley
He makes those movin' doodles on yer magic picture box. He was one of the first inspirations of mine to dip my toes in animation

Jainden
Needs no introduction, Jaiden animations was one of the reasons I went into art.

Carson Teal
Weird (in a great way) Experemental animation

for more animations I love I will create a playlist on youtube in due time

Painting/2Dimensional

Pan Inkhoo
A new fascination of mine, but very cool images nesting in other creatures. very cool.

sixteenflutes
Really vibrant digital paintings. I'm a particular fan of their Hollow Knight work.

Nhi
Beautiful and warm paintings, sculptures, and soft comics. also has a Magazine; Seaglass which seems equal parts beautiful and community building

Kiran Patel
Very beautiful soft shapes and edges

Bonny
In a different vain, her work is more text and image/collage based, more leaning into the philosophy then visual

Pp.png
Beautiful visual painterly style, very unhinged captions.

Greaver
Horror-like illustrations with a similar visual vibe to pp.png

James Gurney
Taught me much of what I know about lighting and working with watercolour and gouache.

Marco Bucci
Also taught me much about painting and a little about blender and 3D

Sinix
Another teacher, mostly of anatomy and digital painting

David Hockney
Specifically the collage work, you can see the influence in my collage work.

Illustration

fern
very fancy fan art, a unique style i've not seen elsewhere

Holls Walck
In a similar vein to Julian Miholics drawings, really cool shapes and compositions of flatter yet complex drawings. They describe themself as an Ecological Folk Artist.

Barna
Also similar to Julian Miholics with dynamic Illustration, small creature sculptures, and vibrant colours.

Sam
More Itch-scratching shapes, but different shapes to the above

Clara Thulin
Really cool illustration work, her shapes scratch my brain in a wonderful way

AMPREH
very beautiful illustration work, watercolour and ink vibes.

Tammsle
Tattoo tickets, but such creative ideas behind them.

Jasmyn Arnold
Wonderful pop colours and elegant subjects

Eli Spencer
Black and white, inked looking Illustration with the most wonderful composition

Morgan
More very pleasing composition

Inika
Wonderfull illustrations and paintings involving vibrant colours and full borders

Helvetica Blanc
A new one to me but with illustrations so unique and interesting and mystic, I feel drawn to their art.

Peter
Really inspiring doodles and recently experiments with photo and sculpture, though he will always be Peter Draws to me.

Chiara Bautista
Lots of skeletons, wolves, pretty people and stardust
