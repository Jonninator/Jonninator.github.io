A Phisical Collage Self Portrait

Essentially this self portrait explored messing around with the percived boundries of gender and what should be seen on whom. I don't really care about the labels and will be me; Fluid.
The official artist statement for the course:
Assigned male at birth, known mostly by male pronouns and my assigned name or typical nicknames out of convenience, In my mind these are simply the labels that are used in conversation to apply to me. These terms hold no weight over what I wear or how I dress, though those things are still restricted by the court of public opinion and my own insecurities when outside of the safety of my home. Fluid is the exploration of that fluidity as it ebbs and floods from day to day, and mirror to mirror that I pass by.
