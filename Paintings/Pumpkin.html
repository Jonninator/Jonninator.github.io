~ Pumpkin and Dripped out Frog ~

Ceramic, Acrylic on Panel

2025

The painting was made as a sort of love letter to this lovely pumpkin sculpture I made in High School that I loved and kept on my dresser with a little frog I gave a chain to and a wooden mushroom a friend carved for me. more personally it is me taking a snapshot of previous version of me and seeing myself in them though I have changed so much.
