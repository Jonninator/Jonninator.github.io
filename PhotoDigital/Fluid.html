~ Fluid ~

Photographic Collage

2024

Assigned male at birth, I was known mostly by male pronouns and my assigned name or typical nicknames out of convenience. In my mind these are simply the labels that are used in conversation to apply to me. These terms hold no weight over what I wear or how I dress, though those things are still restricted by the court of public opinion and my own insecurities when outside of the safety of my home. Fluid is the exploration of that fluidity as it ebbs and floods from day to day and mirror to mirror that I pass by.
