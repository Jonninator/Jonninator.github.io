~ Fishbowl [ UTSC Valley Land Trail ] ~

3D Printed Object, Projected Animation

2024

Fishbowl is a 3D scan of an object in the University of Toronto, Scarborough Campus Valley Land Trail. the object itself is a small post holder to stop traffic. on it was reflective tape that I thought looked like a fish and thus drew a little fish on it. This is the main subject of the Sculpture. brought to light by the sculpture are the fish that pass through the space, hidden behind the crude and rather ugly 3d print and scan, this beautiful scene unfolds like life below our curated natural spaces like the Valley Trail
