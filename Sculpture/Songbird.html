~ Songbird ~

Modified Music Box, Pla Plastic, Mirror Shards, Plastic Beads

2025

A small modified music box fitted with wings, a head, and feet sings a haunting tune when wound. The tiny and incomplex creature is a reflection on the complexity of something so small and fragile as a bird. this metal, plastic, and glass bird despite being the same size as a robin can only mimic her, and sing.
