~ A Locket of my Lovers Hair ~

Thrown Ceramic Pot, Necklace Chain, Flower

2025

I wear pendants frequently, I like having something to visually break up my often monochrome shirts and be more feminine and fun. In the summer I liked to put picked wild-flowers in my pendants to wear, but found they would wither and wilt without water. I made the pot and chain as a solution to this; a way to carry a locket of mother nature's hair with me at all times, as a constant reminder of her kindness and love, as well as how we must treat her and her creatures with kindness and love.
